Brewing Instructions

  1. 1. Start by inspiring passion for innovation at the Illinois Math and Science Academy
  2. 2. Simmer and then develop an entrepreneurship education program
  3. 3. Add in the creativi-tea and move the teapot to Purdue University to study Computer Science
  4. 4. After 1 year, pour the first cup at Microsoft to taste test
  5. 5. If the tea is to your liking, serve and enjoy! If not, try some of these additions:

- If you prefer shaken, not stirred, try adding the flavors of Bharatnatayam, Musical Theater, and Hip Hop

- For a full-bodied drink, the Kanban Challenge incorporates the other skill sets of computer scientists

- Try combining with research into how technology on dancers' bodies can be used to manipulate stage lighting and sound to create an interactive environment
